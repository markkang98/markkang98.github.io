BikeShare Industry

Bike shares are becoming increasingly common in cities in the United States. Commuters have been using bike sharing as a method of transportation for the flexibility, cost savings, exercise, and a myriad of other benefits. While being quite successful, many bike shares are still relatively new and have room for optimization. We will demonstrate data that analyzes and visualizes bike share data from the city of Los Angeles.

Some Important Statistics

What is the average distance?

The average distance each bike share user covers is roughly 4.90 km or 3.04 miles

What are the most popular starting and ending stations?

The most popular starting station is 3069 (34.0508804N, -118.24825W) and the most popular ending station is 3005 (34.0485497 N, -118.25905W).

How many rides were used by people who use bike share as a part of their daily commute?

About 91203 rides were used by people who use bike share as part of their daily commute.

How many rides are used by each type of pass?

81304 rides by Monthly Pass holders, 9517 rides by Flex Pass holders, 382 rides by Staff Annual pass holders
