What are common Passholder Type and Trip Route Category combinations?

The one way trip seems to be larger in every passholder type category. However, the number of round trips made by walk - up users seems to be higher than monthly pass users. This might be the fact that monthly pass users might not hesitate to stop a ride, take care of errands, and rent out another bike to get back to their original destination whereas a new walk- up user would worry about whether they would be able to find another bike to get back to their original destination.
